What is happening?

DeSci.Denver 2024 is a convergence of academics, medical practitioners, technologists, engineers, and science activists building projects in the decentralized science space.

It's a community-facilitated event organized by a decentralized group of contributors initiated by OpSci and The Science Commons Initiative.

Join us as we dive into tech demonstrations, policy development, community engagement, and design workshops.

What can I expect?

You can expect to hear from researchers, web3 natives, DeSci advocates and skeptics, DAO engineers, and projects building tools intended to augment the workflow of researchers.

Themes range from onboarding scientists, the state of DeSci tooling, the future of scientific societies, empowering patient groups, DeSci DAOs, and so much more.

Join us from February 25th through March 2nd for a thrilling exploration of all things DeSci!
